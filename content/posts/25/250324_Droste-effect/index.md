[The droste effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Droste_effect) is the effect of a picture recursively appearing within itself, in a place where a similar picture would realistically be expected to appear.

Not to be confused with the [Droste TV](https://letterboxd.com/film/beyond-the-infinite-two-minutes/).
