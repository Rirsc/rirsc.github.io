{{% project-text %}}
<p>I was part of the 3D team in charge of the developtment of the <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.healthcoda.physera&hl=de&pli=1">Joint & Muscle Health App</a> with guided exercises to reduce user pains. Mainly doing animations, a bit of rigging and a lot of back and forth at it early stages for correct implementation within ViroReact platform. Available now on the Apple Store and Google Play.</p>
