Lucas Pope is back

240312

Return of the Obra Dinn↗ is probably one of the best experiences I had ever have playing a videogame. The art style, the hard puzzles and all the secrets that the history hides. It was also nice cos I played this solo game with friends, so the experience was even cooler. Nothing better than struggling with other brains.
