Lucas Pope is back

241207

Return of the Obra Dinn↗ is probably one of the best experiences I had ever have playing a videogame. The art style, the hard puzzles and all the secrets that history hides. It was also nice cos I played this solo game with friends, so the experience was even cooler. Nothing better than struggling with other brains.

Anyhow, I did this post just to mention the new game from the creator of the Obra; Lucas Pope. Its called Mars After Midnight↗ . I dont own a Playdate, so I wont be able to play it. But the art looks awesome and the approach sounds similar to another of his gems; Papers Please.
