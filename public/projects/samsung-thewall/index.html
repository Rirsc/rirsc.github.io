Samsung

The Wall

In 2019 onformative was commissioned to develop another collection of exclusive digital real-time artworks for Samsung’s latest TV lineup. As a contrast and complementation mutually, the content for the ‘ambient mode’ focuses on kinetic concepts to bring a dynamic dimension to the room.

I had the pleasure of working on this project with the outstanding team at onformative, my role was mostly focused in the creation of the shortfilm for document the project.
