Xiaomi

KS3&K11R

Live Wallpaper and Keyvisuals developed at onformative for Xiaomi and their latest 11T/Pro phone series. These captivating visuals were meticulously developed for Xiaomi’s cutting-edge 11T/Pro phone series.

Our focus centered on the mesmerizing interplay of slow, supercooled liquid movements, creating a dynamic and visually immersive experience that complements the sleek design and advanced features of Xiaomi’s flagship devices.
